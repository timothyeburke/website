Food Truck Notification

Technologies used: JavaScript, node.js, jenkins

The Ringtail Design DC office really loves food trucks. For instance, there are monthly outings during the summer to Truckeroo. Every day at the beginning of lunch time, a jenkins server runs a node script to scrape the food truck data from Food Truck Fiesta, reducing the list using a bounding box filter, and sends an email to the office listing which trucks are outside near the Rosslyn metro station.

Because food trucks. And node. And jenkins.
